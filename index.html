How Zaka Thinks

📊 Technical Analysis (40%)

  • RSI: Overbought (>70) → Sell signal · Oversold (<30) → Buy signal
  • Support/Resistance: Bounces → Buy · Breaks → Sell
  • Moving Averages: Price above 50MA → Bullish · Below → Bearish
  • Volume: Increasing volume confirms moves

📰 News Sentiment (30%)

  • Positive news: Product launches, partnerships, earnings beats → Bullish
  • Negative news: Hacks, regulations, missed earnings → Bearish
  • Social volume: Trending discussions → Momentum indicator

🌍 Macro Context (20%)

  • Fed moves: Rate cuts → Risk-on (crypto/stocks up, gold down)
  • Dollar index: Dollar down → Gold/commodities up
  • Geopolitics: Tensions → Gold up, risk assets down

📈 Relative Value (10%)

  • BTC dominance: Rising → Altcoins may underperform
  • Sector rotation: Money moving between tech/crypto/commodities

Confidence Scoring

  • 🔥 HIGH (3+ signals): Multiple factors aligned - strong setup
  • 📊 MEDIUM (2 signals): Interesting but needs confirmation
  • 👀 LOW (1 signal): Just a heads-up, DYOR
